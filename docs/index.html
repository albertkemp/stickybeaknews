Myna egg thief captured

An Indian Myna egg thief who remains unidentified was captured stealing eggs from an Australian Magpie called Maggie. "We had just gone to do the local shopping and came back with this thief just polishing off one one of our eggs," says Maggie. "We pretty soon chased him off, but he already had one of our babies down his throat."

The BPS (Bird Police Service) is currently in on the investigation. "We can't have thefts like this happening in the country," says Police says Alfred Catchthethief. "We'll do our best to have this case on our hands."

I hope that this new product will be suitable for the customers in need."


Bird baby victim of eagle

A baby bird belonging to the Fluttersnoop family has been likely abducted by an eagle. Bystanders report seeing an eagle making off with a small chick just outside the Fluttersnoops' house.

"I am just devastated!" says Mrs. Fluttersnoop. "He was always an innocent little chick, how wicked of the eagle to take him away." The location of the eagle and chick is still unknown, and the BPS have been inquiring into the exact time when the chick was last seen. "The eagle was mosty brown all over with a black mark under his eye and yellow legs," reports a bystander. "I hope they can soon find the little chick."
